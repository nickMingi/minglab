## Regular Expression

L is a regular language if (a) or (b)
- (a) L can be described by a RE, r
    L consists of only strings generated by r, and no more/less

Even number of a's followed by any # of b's
- L{aabb, aab, aabbb}
- RE: `(aa)*bb * | aa(aa)* b* `
